music portfolio

string arrangements and transcriptions of pop, film, and game music I wrote in my pre-college years.
performed by groups across the country.

music has been a lifelong passion of mine, and I have been using music notation software since I was 10 years old. throughout my middle and high school years I used noteflight and musescore to create string ensemble arrangements of pop, film, and game music- from mashups to medleys to covers with a twist! I have experienced amazing opportunities to perform these arrangements with my friends at school and in the community, as well as conduct my high school's orchestra (performing my studio ghibli medley) and lead an ensemble to play a 10-piece repertoire consisting of mostly my arrangements at the bellavue. I have made my work public so that others can also perform the songs they love- I still find it hard to believe that several musicians across the country used my arrangements in concert!

I have gathered all of my arrangements and transcriptions here in pdf form, including parts, full score, and audio files/links. Please reach out to me at amotas2@gmu.edu if you have any inquiries or plans to perform the arrangement in a public setting!
