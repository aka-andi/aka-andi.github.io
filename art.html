art portfolio

works I've made in the art courses I've taken at gmu as well as digital works and paintings I've made in my free time and for commissions.


drawing has always been a hobby of mine, and I decided to pursue art as a minor to complement all the technical courses I am taking. I had always wanted to learn better art techniques and put myself in an environment where I can explore new ideas, mediums, and styles since most of my drawing as a hobby consisted of doodles with just a pen and paper. below is a portfolio of the work I have done in my art courses, as well as others I have done in my free time.

you can find more of my art and see my process on my instagram: @andiiuki. I am also open to discussing commissions- just email me at amotas2@gmu.edu or send a message on instagram.
